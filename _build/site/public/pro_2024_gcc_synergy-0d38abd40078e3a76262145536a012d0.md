## **Digital Solutions for Urban Decarbonization in İzmir**

**Project Overview** GCC-SYNERGY is a high-impact pilot initiative under the European Union’s **NetZeroCities** program, specifically designed to accelerate İzmir’s transition toward its 2030 climate-neutral goal. The project addresses electricity consumption in buildings, facilities, and infrastructure—which accounts for nearly one-third of the city's carbon emissions—by leveraging real-time monitoring, digital twins, and multi-stakeholder engagement.

* **Project Status:** Ongoing (60% Completed)
* **Timeline:** 2024 – 2026
* **Core Objectives:**
    * Establishment of a **Digital Twin** for a pilot neighborhood to simulate and optimize local energy dynamics.
    * Development of an integrated digital platform for real-time monitoring and management of electricity consumption.
    * Fostering a collaborative ecosystem (**Global Climate Community İzmir**) connecting consumers, renewable energy producers, and decision-makers.
    * Creation of a policy and incentive framework to promote sustainable behavioral change through data-driven insights.
* **Project Partners:** İzmir Metropolitan Municipality (Lead Coordinator), Ege University, İzmir Institute of Technology (IZTECH), and various local energy stakeholders.
* **Expected Outputs:**
    * A comprehensive **Decision Support System (DSS)** platform for urban energy management.
    * Interactive solar energy potential maps for residential and public rooftops.
    * A citizen training portal focused on energy efficiency and climate literacy.
    * Policy recommendations and guidelines for scalable carbon footprint mitigation strategies.
* **My Role & Contribution:**
    * Leading the work packages for **Geographic Information Systems (GIS)** and **Decision Support Systems (DSS)** development.
    * **Scientific Focus:** Utilizing **GIS and Remote Sensing** technologies to estimate **rooftop Solar PV potential** at the neighborhood scale, providing the technical foundation for the project’s Digital Twin.
    * Managing spatial data integration, stakeholder mapping, and ensuring scientific accuracy in technical reporting and carbon modeling.
