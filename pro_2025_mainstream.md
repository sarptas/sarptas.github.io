**Project Overview**
MAINSTREAM is a transnational research project funded under the **Water4All 2024 Joint Transnational Call**. The project aims to revolutionize industrial water circularity by overcoming technological, economic, and social barriers to water reuse. By integrating innovative sensor technologies, cost-effective treatment processes, and digitalization tools, MAINSTREAM targets a "Zero Liquid Discharge" (ZLD) goal within industrial ecosystems.


* **Project Status:** Ongoing (Active Implementation Phase)
* **Timeline:** 2024 – 2027
* **Core Objectives:**
    * To promote water circularity in the industrial sector and ensure the sustainable management of water resources.
    * To develop and implement innovative, low-cost sensors for real-time monitoring of pollutants in industrial wastewater.
    * To establish advanced treatment and recovery processes designed for industrial symbiosis models.
    * To create digital decision-support tools and governance mechanisms that facilitate the transition to circular water economies.

**Pilot Site Focus**
The project focuses on high-impact case studies, notably in the **Küçük Menderes Basin (Torbalı region)** in Türkiye—a region significantly affected by water scarcity and industrial pollution. This pilot serves as a benchmark for implementing industrial water reuse strategies in Mediterranean climates.

**My Role & Contribution**
* **Work Package Responsibility:** Leading the tasks within **Work Package 5 (WP5)**, specifically focused on **"Water Flow Analysis and Mapping."**
* **Scientific Specialization:** Applying **Geographic Information Systems (GIS)** expertise to model and visualize industrial water flows and spatial distribution of water-related risks.
* **Impact Assessment:** Utilizing spatial analysis to identify potential areas for industrial symbiosis and to assess the regional impact of wastewater reuse on water balance.

**Expected Impacts**
* **Technological Innovation:** Validation of new sensors and cost-effective treatment systems in real industrial environments.
* **Environmental Sustainability:** Reduction of freshwater withdrawal by industrial zones and mitigation of wastewater discharge into sensitive ecosystems.
* **Strategic Governance:** Development of a comprehensive roadmap for policy-makers and industrial park managers to scale up water circularity practices across Europe and the Mediterranean.
